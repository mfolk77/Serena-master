This directory contains all marketing materials and assets for SerenaNet's App Store submission and promotional activities.

## App Store Requirements

### Screenshots (Required)
- **Main Interface**: 1280x800 or 1440x900
- **Voice Input Demo**: 1280x800 or 1440x900  
- **Settings & Privacy**: 1280x800 or 1440x900
- **Conversation Management**: 1280x800 or 1440x900
- **Help & Onboarding**: 1280x800 or 1440x900

### App Preview Video (Optional but Recommended)
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds
- **Format**: MP4, MOV, or M4V
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Content**: Feature demonstration with clear value proposition

### App Icons
- **macOS**: 1024x1024px PNG (no transparency)
- **All sizes**: 16x16, 32x32, 128x128, 256x256, 512x512 (1x and 2x)
- **Design**: Professional, recognizable, Apple guidelines compliant

## Key Messaging

### Primary Value Proposition
"Private AI assistant that runs entirely on your device"

### Key Benefits
1. **Complete Privacy**: All processing happens locally
2. **Offline Capability**: Works without internet connection
3. **Native Experience**: Built specifically for macOS
4. **Voice Input**: Hands-free interaction
5. **Secure Storage**: Encrypted conversations

### Target Audience
- **Privacy-conscious users**
- **Professionals handling sensitive information**
- **Students and researchers**
- **Mac enthusiasts**
- **AI early adopters**

## Content Guidelines

### Do's
- ✅ Emphasize privacy and local processing
- ✅ Highlight native macOS integration
- ✅ Show real use cases and benefits
- ✅ Use clear, jargon-free language
- ✅ Include accessibility features
- ✅ Demonstrate offline capabilities

### Don'ts
- ❌ Compare directly to other AI assistants
- ❌ Make unrealistic performance claims
- ❌ Use technical jargon without explanation
- ❌ Show placeholder or incomplete features
- ❌ Include any tracking or analytics mentions
- ❌ Reference unimplemented features

## Asset Creation Checklist

### Screenshots
- [ ] Clean, professional interface
- [ ] Realistic conversation examples
- [ ] Proper lighting and contrast
- [ ] No personal information visible
- [ ] Consistent theme/appearance
- [ ] High resolution and sharp

### App Preview Video
- [ ] Clear narration or text overlay
- [ ] Smooth transitions and animations
- [ ] Feature highlights in logical order
- [ ] Professional audio quality
- [ ] Proper pacing (not too fast/slow)
- [ ] Strong opening and closing

### App Icons
- [ ] Scalable design (works at all sizes)
- [ ] Unique and memorable
- [ ] Follows Apple design guidelines
- [ ] Professional appearance
- [ ] Consistent with app theme
- [ ] No text or fine details

## Review and Approval Process

1. **Content Creation**: Marketing team creates initial materials
2. **Technical Review**: Development team validates accuracy
3. **Design Review**: Design team ensures brand consistency
4. **Legal Review**: Legal team approves all claims and descriptions
5. **Final Approval**: Product owner signs off on all materials
6. **App Store Submission**: Materials uploaded to App Store Connect
